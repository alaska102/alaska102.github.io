In the summer of 2021, I taught low-income rising 2nd graders in the Bay Area as part of an initiative called The Big Lift, which attempts to minimize education inequalities. I facilitated STEM projects, such as building robots, doing science experiments, and taught math. Additionally, I allocated resources for the program, to ensure scholars could learn as much as possible, in an attempt to reduce summer learning loss.
